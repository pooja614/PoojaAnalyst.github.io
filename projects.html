Ecommerce Churn Analysis

Churn analytics helps to detect product friction points and understand the reasons behind churn.
Churn analysis helps to understand the focus area for improvement:
  • Customer onboarding
  • Product usability.
Here are the most common reasons why customers stop paying for your product, according to the Zopka study:
  • Poor Onboarding: Poor and non-personalized product tours and GUI issues and popups.
  • Poor customer service: Timely customer support, in-app help centers are important.
  • Unfullfilled Expectations: Apt description of the product resembling actual functionality.
  • High Pricing: Aligning pricing line to the market and product features offered is crucial.
Taking proactive measures towards preventing churn will ensure growth of the business.
